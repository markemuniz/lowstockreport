How to Pull the Report from Dutchie

  1. Open Dutchie Back Office.
  2. On the left, navigate to Products, then select Inventory.
  3. Click the Rooms button and select only DAY VAULT, Sales Floor, and VAULT.
  4. Go to the Actions dropdown and select Export.
  5. Leave the export selections as they are and export the file.
  6. Upload that file below, where it says “Drop the inventory CSV here, or click to browse.”
